## Introducing ScholarMap

I'm excited to share **ScholarMap** - a platform that maps global biomedical research opportunities by analyzing 36+ million PubMed publications.

Instead of searching for papers, ScholarMap answers the crucial question: **"Where are the researchers?"**

## How It Works

ScholarMap uses AI to:

1. **Parse your research interests** into comprehensive PubMed queries
2. **Extract geographic data** from author affiliations across millions of publications
3. **Map researchers** by country, city, and institution
4. **Visualize the landscape** with an interactive 3D map

**[PLACEHOLDER: Infographic showing the 4-step process]**

The result? A comprehensive view of where your field is most active, helping you make informed decisions about your next career move.
